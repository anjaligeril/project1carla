Carla is an old teacher with a new attitude. She has been teaching children for three decades (and that means 30 years since a decade is 10 years!*), but Carla has never been old-fashioned. Carla's new classroom is completely online. Lessons change all the time so check back often for new material.

Carla teaches the 3 Rs**. Pick a topic, click on the link, and get started!

Carla's motto: Never miss a chance to teach -- and to learn!!
